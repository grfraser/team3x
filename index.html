The Team

Charlie Brown

Two years after high school our mom bought us our first Mac in 1995. I attempted to write my first HTML page and failed. 23 years later I’m developing web application software well beyond mywebpage.html. After graduating from college with an engineering degree I ventured into NYC and worked for some of the top recording studios. My web dreams weren’t as strong. But I had a tremendous background and knowledge of ripping computers apart and putting them back together even better. I used that skill a long time in many different IT positions. Landing an A+ cert doing tech support around the country. I chased a passion a for music but an even a deeper passion for the web. I got more technical and attained several positions implementing big data distributed systems as a Certified SAP Business Objects Architect in several software packages. I served Fortune 500 company's enterprise applications that sprung from canned solutions technology. After a small stint in the corporate world I decided to follow my dream and become a web developer. I went to a coding bootcamp, already having coded on my own for 1 year. I did the cohort for the polish. Know that I hope to put that polish on your project. I love Skyping with my daughter, a late night run; cooking to me is therapeutic, meditating, and hanging out with my family.
